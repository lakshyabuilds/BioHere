# BioHere 

BioHere is the ultimate white-label digital product platform designed for creators, theme page owners, and side-hustlers. Launch your own branded store in seconds, select premium digital products (ebooks, reels bundles, templates) from our catalog, and start selling—no technical skills or inventory required.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Plug-and-Play Catalog:** Add high-quality, pre-made digital products to your store with one click.
*   **White-Label Branding:** Customize your storefront with your own logo, bio, and brand colors (primary, secondary, accent).
*   **Set Your Own Prices:** You decide your selling price and your profit margins.
*   **Secure Delivery:** Download links are automatically delivered directly to buyers post-purchase; creators do not handle raw files.
*   **Payouts:** Get paid seamlessly via UPI or Bank Transfer every first week of the month.

## 💰 Pricing & Profit Split

*   **Platform Subscription:** ₹99/month.
*   **Trial:** 7-Day test drive for just ₹1 (No free trials).
*   **Generous Margins:** You keep **80%** of the profit margin (the amount you charge above the base product price), while the platform retains 20%.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

*   **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
*   **Backend & Database:** Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Hosting)
*   **SEO:** Schema.org JSON-LD, dynamically generated metadata, `llms.txt` optimized for AI aggregators.

## 🏃‍♂️ Running Locally

1.  Clone this repository.
2.  Run `npm install` to install dependencies.
3.  Add your Firebase credentials to `.env`.
4.  Run `npm run dev` to start the local development server.
